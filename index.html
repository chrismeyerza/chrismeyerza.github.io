fodmap-guru

A personalised low-FODMAP food tool. The science of low-FODMAP is well-documented, but the practical operational layer — knowing what to buy, what to eat, what to skip — is missing. fodmap-guru pairs a food preferences questionnaire with supermarket product filtering to surface safe, personalised options.
